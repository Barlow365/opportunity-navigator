**Goal:** Validate three-layer system works without bailouts

| Feature | Effort | Priority |

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Reserve depletion** | Auto-tightening, helper-first routing |
| **Legal issues** | Legal review, voluntary repayment |
| **Helper fatigue** | Recognition, limits, no pressure |
| **System abuse** | Community credit gates access |

---

## Decision Points

### After MVP (Week 15)

**Criteria:**
- [ ] 100+ users, 30+ helpers
- [ ] 70%+ match rate
- [ ] 80%+ repayment rate
- [ ] No liquidity crisis
- [ ] Positive sentiment

**If YES:** Proceed to nonprofit partnerships
**If NO:** Iterate on trust system
