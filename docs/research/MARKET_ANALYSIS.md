## Regulatory Landscape

### CFPB Small-Dollar Loan Rule (2025)

**Requirements:**
- Assess ability to repay
- Clear disclosures
- Limit rollovers
- Cap withdrawal attempts

**Opportunity Navigator Compliance:**
- ✅ Not legally enforceable debt (voluntary repayment)
- ✅ No interest charged
- ✅ Small amounts ($10-$25)
- ✅ Clear terms ("this is not a loan")

### Europe: Consumer Credit Directive (CCD2)

**Requirements:**
- Standardized pre-contractual info
- Affordability assessments
- Ban on dark patterns

**Opportunity Navigator:**
- If expanding to EU, full compliance required
- Voluntary nature simplifies compliance
